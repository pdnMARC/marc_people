Kavinda Dilshara Herath is a Research Assistant and Project Coordinator at the
Multidisciplinary AI Research Centre (MARC), University of Peradeniya. He holds
a **B.Sc. Engineering (Hons) in Electrical and Information Engineering** from the
University of Ruhuna, graduating with **First-Class Honours**. His research interests span **Computer vision, deep learning, and signal processing**. He has a strong track record of research excellence, with multiple publications in top-tier journals and conferences, including an upcoming paper in the *IEEE Sensors Journal* (IF: 4.5, Q1). Dilshara's work has been recognized with several prestigious awards, such as the **International Winners** title in the IEEE IES Generative AI Challenge 2025 and the **1st Place (National)** award in the IEEE Innovations Sri Lanka Competition 2024.
